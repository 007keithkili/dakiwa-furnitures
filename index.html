Rooted in Kenya,
crafted for the world.

Dakiwa was born from a love for honest materials and timeless design. Every piece is handcrafted in our Nairobi workshop, using sustainably sourced hardwoods and traditional joinery techniques passed down through generations.

We believe furniture should not only be beautiful but also tell a story — of the land it came from, the hands that shaped it, and the home it will become part of.
